The Origin

I didn't come from a traditional tech background. I came from curiosity, necessity, and a refusal to stay stuck. I taught myself to code because I had problems I needed to solve — and once I started, I couldn't stop.

Over time, one skill turned into many. Web development led to AI. IT led to cybersecurity. A passion for healing led to a massage therapy license. A desire to share led to content creation. None of it was planned. All of it was intentional.

Today I build technology for real organizations, real clients, and real people — websites that perform, tools that work, and systems that last. I also believe in healing the whole person, which is why Sacred Hands exists alongside everything else I do.
